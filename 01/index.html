Business Situation

A growing company has $10,000 available for one initiative this year. Leaders are comparing three options. The Finance lens asks which choice creates value over time, what assumptions must hold, and who carries the downside if the choice fails.

The company requires a 10% return. The present value formula is shown in words: future benefit divided by one plus the required return.
