Business Situation

A company is considering a new credit-based subscription plan. It could increase revenue and customer access, but the plan depends on customers paying on time, the platform working reliably, and public trust staying strong.

Your job is to analyze the decision through Finance: classify the risks, identify who carries the downside, and choose safeguards before the company launches.
