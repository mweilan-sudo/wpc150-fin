Responsible Finance Recommendation Builder

Create a finance recommendation by setting priorities, choosing a funding approach, and selecting evidence that would help leaders monitor the decision after launch.

Business Situation

A company can expand a student-focused service into a new city. The expansion could create long-term value, but it requires money now, creates uncertainty, and may affect employees, customers, and community partners.

Your task is to build a recommendation that does more than chase the largest possible return. A strong finance recommendation explains the tradeoff, protects liquidity, and names evidence leaders should watch.

Set Recommendation Priorities

Use the sliders to create a priority profile from 1 to 5. A defensible recommendation should not maximize every priority at once. It should show judgment about tradeoffs.
